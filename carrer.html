Academic Timeline & Important Dates

Stay updated with admission dates, entrance exams, and scholarship deadlines.

March - April
Class 12 Board Results
Most state boards and CBSE release Class 12 results. Start preparing for college applications immediately after results.
April - May
College Application Period
Most universities open their application process. Research colleges, prepare documents, and apply early for better chances.
May - June
Entrance Exams
JEE Main, NEET, CLAT, and other entrance exams are conducted. Prepare well and register on time.
June - July
Merit Lists & Counseling
Universities release merit lists and start counseling process. Keep all documents ready for verification.
July - August
Admissions & Classes Begin
Final admissions are completed and new academic session starts. Complete all formalities on time.
Throughout the Year
Scholarship Applications
Various scholarships open throughout the year. Keep checking government and private scholarship portals regularly.
October - December
Next Year Preparation
Start preparing for next year's entrance exams if you're in Class 11 or planning to retake exams.

Important Reminders

  • Keep all documents ready: Mark sheets, certificates, ID proofs
  • Apply for scholarships early - many have limited seats
  • Stay in touch with college admission offices for updates
  • Regularly check university websites for important announcements
  • Set up notifications for application deadlines
